How to Position Your Fingers

1. Place your fingers on the home row keys (highlighted with a glow).
2. Left hand: Place your fingers on A (pinky), S (ring), D (middle), and F (index).
3. Right hand: Place your fingers on J (index), K (middle), L (ring), and ; (pinky).
4. Keep your thumbs hovering over the spacebar.
5. Use the finger with the matching color to press each key.
6. Return to the home position after pressing keys away from the home row.
